	Here are the list of commands that I support
	help - List of commands supported by me, Friday.
	list - View all entries to the current list of things you have asked me to take note of.
	mark <index> - Mark an entry in the list as a completed task.
	unmark <index> - Unmark an entry in the list as a completed task.
	todo <description> - Remember a TODO Task for you to revisit again.
	deadline <description> /by <when> - Remember a Deadline Task for you to complete by the deadline.
	event <description> /from <when> /to <when> - Remember an Event Task from when it begins to when it ends.
	delete <index> - Delete an entry from your current list.
	bye - Exits this app and says Good Bye to Friday :)
